About Us

En·light·en

/inˈlītn/

verb

  1. give (someone) greater knowledge and understanding about a subject or situation

At Enlighten.org, we believe that understanding is the first step toward change. Our mission is to improve the quality of education in America by shining a light on the issues that affect our schools, classrooms, and communities. Through interactive state-based insights, we help users discover the educational challenges and initiatives that matter most where they live. Whether you arrive curious or concerned, we want every visitor to leave feeling informed, inspired, and truly enlightened about the state of education—and empowered to be part of the solution.
