Why should you care?

Education is the foundation for economic mobility, community vitality, and civic participation. Strong schools attract employers, reduce inequality, and build resilience. When education falters, the costs ripple across generations.

You can help by engaging with your local schools, supporting educators, and advocating for policies that expand access to high-quality learning experiences for every student.
